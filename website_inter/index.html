    • A tribute to

    Dr Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar
    (Dr Babasaheb Ambedkar)


    1891-1956



    Jay Bhim, Dr Babasaheb Ambedkar is the Chief Architect of Indian Constitution, he was a scholar par
    excellence, a philosopher, a visionary, an emancipator ,a historian,a true nationalist ,a Bodhisattva
    and many more. He led a number of social movements to secure human rights to the oppressed and
    depressed sections of the society and womens . He stands as a symbol of struggle for
    social justice .
